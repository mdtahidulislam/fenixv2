Our Mission

Fenix is a private membership network to support
Asian American Women executives in the investment industry. We provide access to curated capital
connections, events, and executive advancement opportunities in support of diversity initiatives.

Fenix serves as the catalyst for changing the diversity
and business landscape.

Our Team

Fenix is founded by two Asian American women that have
first-hand experience of the demands of being an investor, a fund partner, and an entrepreneur.

They came together on their passions to advance AAPI women as leaders in industries where AAPI are underrepresented.

Diane Yoo

Shveta Pillai

Benifits
  • Investor Networking. Institutional, Family Officers, and High Net Worth Investor support women led funds.
  • Executive Advancement. C-suite and board seat opportunities.
  • Fenix Circles. Intimate events with other high performing AAW Investment Managers.
  • Executive Coaching. Executive and leadership coaching to achieve next level performance.
  • Fen-Well Programs. Programming on topics impacting a women's career, health, and wellness.
